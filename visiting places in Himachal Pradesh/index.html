Best Places to visit

Manali

Hidimba Devi Temple, Solang Valley, Mall Road Manali, Sissu, Lahul, Atal Tunnel,Jogini Waterfall, Solang Nullah, International Roerich Memorial Trust, Bhrigu Lake, Manali Sanctuary, Manu Market, Rani Nala.

Kullu

Hamta Pass, Great Himalayan National Park, Tirthan Valley, Bijli Mahadev Temple, Jalori Pass, Hot springs at Manikaran, Vaishno Devi Temple, Hanogi Mata Temple, Dechan Choekhor Mahavihara Monastery

Chamba

Khajjiar Lake, Kalatop wildlife sanctuary,Chamunda Devi Temple, Chamera Lake, Dalhousie, Manimahesh Lake, Bhuri Singh Museum, Akhand Chandi Palace, Champavati Temple, Lakshmi Narayan Temple.

Govind Sagar Lake, Bilaspur

One can enjoy multiple sports activities in the reservoir like swimming, surfing, water-skiing, kayaking, rowing, canoeing, and white water river rafting.

Atal Tannel, Rohtang

Atal Tunnel is also referred to as the Rohtang Tunnel, It is a horseshoe-shaped highway tunnel that connects Manali and the Lahaul-Spiti valley.

Palampur

It's known for its tea gardens like the Palampur Cooperative Tea Factory, which also processes the leaves. A waterfall drops into a stream at Bundla Chasm. Saurabh Van Vihar nature park has picnic spots, birdlife and views of the snow-capped Dhauladhar range.

Mandi

Lord Shiva as the main deity of Bhootnath, Trilokinath, Panchvaktra and other important temples has ordained Mandi as the 'Varanasi of the Hills' as 'Choti Kashi'. For its temple architecture, old palace and rich traditions, Mandi is so often considered as the cultural capital of the state.

Shimla

Shimla is famous for its scenic beauty, pleasant climate, colonial architecture, mall road, adventure activites and historical significance. It is the capital city of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh and is situated in the foothills of the Himalayas.

Bilaspur

Bilaspur in Himachal Pradesh is famous for its Bhakra Dam, Gobind Sagar Lake, Naina Devi Temple, rich cultural heritage, adventure tourism, and religious tourism, making it a popular tourist destination in the state.

Kinnaur

Kinnaur is famous for Baspa river, Sarhan, Sangla valley, Kalpa, Ribba, Borasu pass,Nichar, Suicide point, Kamru fort, Recongpeo, Namgia, Lipa Asrang Sanctuary, Rupin Pass, Parikarma, Nako lake.

Kangra

Kangra is famous for Dharamshala, Kangra Fort, Masroor Rock Cut Temple, Brajeshwari Devi Temple, Kareri Lake, Indrahar Pass Trek, Dhauladhar National Park, Chinmay Tapovan, Minkiani Pass, Lakshami Narayan Temple,Baijnath Temple, Jawala Devi Temple, Chamunda Devi Temple, Masrur, Tashijong, Pong Dam.

Una

Una is famous for Chintpurani Temple, Sheelta Devi Temple, Baba Rudranand Ashram, Dera Baba Bharbhag Singh, Pir Nigah, Shiva Bari, Guru Govind Singh Lake, bombay-picnic spot.
